Short Bio

Hey! I am Nahid from Bangladesh. Recently, I have completed my masters' in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at the Oklahoma State University, advised by Prof. Rushikesh Kamalapurkar. Currently, I am working as a Research Engineer at the Systems, Cognition, and Control Laboratory, Oklahoma State University (OSU). During my masters', I worked as a Graduate Research Assistant/Graduate Teaching Assistant in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering of OSU. Besides that, I am serving as the President of the Bangladeshi Student Association (BSA) at OSU. I have also served as the Public Relations officer of the Muslim Student Association (MSA) at OSU as well as the Student Representative for the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) to the CEAT Student Council at OSU.
